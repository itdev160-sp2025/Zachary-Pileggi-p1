Sakamoto Days

Chapters



Sakamoto Days is a fun, innovative and action-packed story. Though it may sound familiar to stories such as the John Wick series, where Sakamoto Days stands out is by taking a more comedic approach. Author Yuto Suzuki expertly blends lighthearted comedy and captivating action. Theres also one more twist. Taro Sakamoto has promised to his wife that he will never take another person's life ever again. As hit men and assassins come after Sakamoto day-in and day-out, he must come up with creative solutions on the fly in order to pacify the trouble at hand and maintain his peaceful life.
